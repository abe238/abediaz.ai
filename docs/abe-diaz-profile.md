## Professional Summary

A passionate technologist and humanitarian leader with 15+ years of experience spanning enterprise software, mobile platforms, and logistics innovation. Career highlights include building mobile apps at **Deloitte** and **NBCUniversal**, optimizing revenue systems at **Amazon Prime Video**, and now leading Amazon's global disaster relief operations.

**The Pivot (2017):** Born and raised in Puerto Rico, Abe was compelled to act when Hurricane Maria devastated his homeland. He volunteered to lead Amazon's first relief plane to the island—an experience he describes as "life-changing." This led to his permanent transition to the Disaster Relief team.

**Mission:** Leveraging Amazon's logistics and technology for humanitarian good.
